I have things narrowed down to three basic font families:

  • Monospace - Consolas
  • Serif - Garamond
  • Sans Serif - Archivo

Consolas is a proprietary font from Microsoft. I have uploaded it to the /fonts/ folder on this site to make sure it is available. A free alternative is Inconsolata from Google, which has the advantage of having variable width and weight, but the disadvantage of having no italic version.

The version of Garamond I like is from Adobe, Garamond Premier Pro. It includes a very nice italic based on historic fonts by Robert Granjon.

Archivo is a free font from Google which is fully variable. This is one of its best features. This font won out in an intense head-to-head battle with Neue Helvetica and Arial Black to be featured in the current Pop Empire logo.
